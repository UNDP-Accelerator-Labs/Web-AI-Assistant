Iran Air Flight 655

Iran Air Flight 655 was a scheduled passenger flight from Tehran to Dubai via Bandar Abbas that was shot down on July 3, 1988, by two surface-to-air missiles fired by the USS Vincennes, a United States Navy warship. The missiles hit the Iran Air aircraft, an Airbus A300, while it was flying its usual route over Iran's territorial waters in the Persian Gulf.

The incident resulted in the loss of life of 290 innocent people on board. The passenger plane had been incorrectly identified as a fighter jet by the USS Vincennes, which led to the tragic accident.

Battle of the Winwaed

The Battle of the Winwaed was fought on November 15th, 655 AD, between King Penda of Mercia and King Oswiu of Northumbria. The Mercians were outnumbered by the Northumbrians, who emerged victorious.
